Dubai

A city of superlatives, Dubai is famous for iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and the spectacular Dubai Fountain. Take a scenic cruise on the Dubai Creek and enjoy popular Emirati dishes such as Shawarma, Majboos, and traditional Arabic coffee.

Abu Dhabi

The capital city offers a mix of heritage and modernity with attractions like the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Ferrari World, and Yas Island. Indulge in fresh seafood and local delicacies while exploring luxurious resorts and cultural sites.

Sharjah

Known as the cultural capital, Sharjah boasts beautiful museums, art galleries, and the serene Corniche Walk. Experience traditional markets and enjoy treats like Luqaimat and Khuzi while soaking in the city’s artistic vibe.

Fujairah

Nestled along the Gulf of Oman, Fujairah is famous for its stunning beaches, rugged mountains, and historic forts. It’s a perfect destination for snorkeling, diving, and exploring natural beauty. Sample local dishes like Madrouba and fresh seafood while enjoying the peaceful coastal atmosphere.
